How it works

Four steps to your first playable game.

01

Pick a game idea

Choose an obby, tycoon, simulator, roleplay map, or a totally new mini-game.

02

Build the world

Use parts, terrain, colors, materials, and lighting to make your game feel real.

03

Code with Lua

Write scripts for coins, checkpoints, doors, timers, enemies, and power-ups.

04

Playtest and share

Invite friends, fix bugs, publish your experience, and keep improving it.
