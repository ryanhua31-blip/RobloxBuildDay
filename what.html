What is Build Day?

Build Day turns Roblox players into creators.

Build Day is an online event where Roblox players learn how games are made inside Roblox Studio. Mentors guide participants through planning, building, scripting, testing, and publishing a small playable experience.

Roblox Studio Lua lessons Playable prototype

The goal

Turn curiosity into creator confidence.

For players

Build Day shows that the games players love are made from simple parts, scripts, and ideas.

For new builders

Participants learn Roblox Studio basics without needing to already feel like developers.

For future creators

The event helps builders leave with skills they can keep using after the hackathon ends.
