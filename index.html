What is Build Day?

A friendly jump from player to creator.

Build Day is an online event where Roblox players learn how games are made inside Roblox Studio. Mentors guide participants through planning, building, scripting, testing, and publishing a small playable experience.

How it works

Four steps to your first playable game.

01

Pick a game idea

Choose an obby, tycoon, simulator, roleplay map, or a totally new mini-game.

02

Build the world

Use parts, terrain, colors, materials, and lighting to make your game feel real.

03

Code with Lua

Write scripts for coins, checkpoints, doors, timers, enemies, and power-ups.

04

Playtest and share

Invite friends, fix bugs, publish your experience, and keep improving it.
